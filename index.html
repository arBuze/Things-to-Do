Things you can do, when you don't know what to do.

Did you ever have a feeling, that you want to do something: to eat, to watch, to work with, but you just don't know what? Then you can look through this site and maybe you will find something interesting.

  • Pet

    Pat your pet

    Aren't they adorable? Take some photos of your pet(s) and send them to friends.

  • Coffee break

    Have a snack

    Food is good, and maybe after a snack your brain will figure out what to do.

  • Walk down the street

    Go for a walk

    Go wherever you want, and you might find something new. Walking is also good for your health.

  • Chat

    Talk with friends

    Message your friend, talk about how your day went and what happened.

  • Reading

    Read a book

    Continue reading a book or pick up a new one and dive into it.

  • Hobby

    Do your hobby

    Make something, create something of your own choice.

  • Cooking

    Cook something

    Seen a video of a delicious meal? Maybe it's time to try it!

  • Game

    Play a game

    Turn on computer, find a game that fits your mood and begin.

  • Work

    Go to work :(

    No matter how sad it is, you need to do important things too, else you won't have enough time for that.
